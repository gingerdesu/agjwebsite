Greater China Club -Wharton

[2014] Logo Design Project

Greater China Club is the Chinese student organization at Wharton School of Business at University of Pennsylvania. This is my first time receiving a request for clients outside of family. The first character of the logo is designed to look like the brush for caligraphy, and the rest of the letter paints the outline of the map of China.
