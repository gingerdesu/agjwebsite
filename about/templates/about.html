Hi, my name is Annie.

I am a chemist, designer in making, and collector of experiences.

"Be thankful for everything that happens in your life; it's all an experience"

Currently finishing my senior year of Bachelors of Science in Specialized Chemistry and minor in Applied Statistics at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign.

I was born in Glendale, California, grew up in Shenyang, China until 7th grade, grew up (part 2) in Southern California, and finally moved to the Midwest for college. Thanks to these experiences, I was able to absorb different lifestyles, morals, views, and social structures and really appreciate the different ways individuals think based on their backgrounds.

Some of the most random skills I have include contributing to Urban Dictionary and accidentally winning a province-wide trivia competition. Let's chat and learn more about each other!
